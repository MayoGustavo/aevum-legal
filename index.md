# Privacy Policy for Aevum

**Last Updated:** 04/28/2026

Aevum ("we", "our", or "us") is dedicated to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the Aevum mobile application (the "App").

## 1. The Core Philosophy: Your Wisdom is Yours
Aevum is designed as an offline-first compendium. **We do not require you to create an account, and we do not have a centralized database that stores your personal data.** All of your saved quotes, journal entries, and reflections are stored locally on your physical device. We cannot read them, we do not have access to them, and we do not sell them.

## 2. Information We Do Collect (Crash Reporting)
To ensure the Forge runs smoothly and without errors, we use a third-party service called Sentry for crash reporting and performance monitoring. 

If the App crashes, Sentry automatically collects anonymous diagnostic data. This data does *not* include your personal journal entries or saved quotes. It strictly includes technical information such as:
* Your device model and operating system version.
* The time of the crash and the specific line of code that failed.
* Your device's IP address (temporarily processed to determine the geographic region of the error, but not stored in a way that identifies you).

## 3. Third-Party Services
We do not use advertising trackers or marketing analytics. Sentry is the only third-party service integrated into the App. You can review Sentry's privacy policy at [https://sentry.io/privacy/](https://sentry.io/privacy/).

## 4. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
